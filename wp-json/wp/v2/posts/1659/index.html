In June 2022, the National Bank of Ukraine fined Concord Bank \u20b460.4 million. A year later, in August 2023, the regulator revoked its license<\/a> and shut down JSC “AKB Concord”. This decision was based on the results of official inspections, which showed that the bank regularly violated laws on anti-money laundering, countering terrorism financing, and preventing the spread of weapons of mass destruction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

According to reports, Concord Bank processed around 25% of Ukraine\u2019s gambling market \u2014 most of it operating without paying taxes. The bank used a scheme called miscoding, where transactions were incorrectly labeled to hide their true purpose. This allowed businesses to avoid taxes and caused huge losses to the state budget every year.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
