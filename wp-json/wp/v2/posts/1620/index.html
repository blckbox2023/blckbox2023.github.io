Let\u2019s start with Cherkaskyi\u2019s official asset declaration. Ironically, the man responsible for monitoring the country’s financial flows has gone out of his way to make monitoring his own assets nearly impossible. Who better than Ukraine\u2019s top financial watchdog to know how to hide money and property?<\/p>\n\n\n\n

For example, Cherkaskyi does not disclose how much he paid for a luxury car collection registered under his wife\u2019s name. All these cars were bought brand new and likely cost several hundred thousand dollars in total.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
